Roller Calc Privacy Policy

Last update: Jan 21, 2023

At Roller Calc we recognize that privacy is important. This policy has been created to explain our privacy practices, so that you understand what information about you is collected and how it is used.

By using our application, you agree to the terms and practices described in this policy. We reserve the right to change, modify or alter this policy at any time. You will be notified of any changes, but it is recommended to consult this Policy regularly in case you do not receive the notification.

1. WHAT TYPE OF INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal Information

Access Token. In order to use the Refresh Feature, we require your RollerCoin token. (Please note that this feature is optional)

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Personal Information

Access Token. Your RollerCoin token is used to refresh data using your real data at your request. It is encrypted and stored on your device in the app's internal data folder (never sent to external servers).

Note: the token is used solely and exclusively to request data from the RollerCoin server, never to modify or delete data.
